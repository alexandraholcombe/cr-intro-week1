About Me

My name is Allie Holcombe, and that is not a picture of me. I was unable to find any on this PC, so I am temporarily using a sim I made back when I was super into the Sims 3.

I come to Epicodus from a retail sales leadership position and most of an undergrad degree in history. In January, a friend of mine with a degree in theater tech started working at a startup as a junior developer. When I asked how she got there, she told me about her experiences at a coding bootcamp. For the first time since leaving college, a new path forward caught my attention.

"I can do that," I thought. "I used to enjoy HTML and CSS so much!" Unfortunately, with college work and a full-time job, my time spent learning how to make Wordpress themes and figuring out how Joomla worked dwindled to no time at all.

After several months of investigating bootcamps and learn-to-code programs in Washington, California, and Colorado, as well as studying Javascript to pass one of the admissions exams, I found Epicodus. The flipped-classroom and pairing model sold me.
